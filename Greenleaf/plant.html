About GreenLeaf by Araj

GreenLeaf promotes sustainable gardening and eco-friendly lifestyles. We provide a variety of indoor and outdoor plants to help you build a green space at home or in your community.

Our Popular Plants

Peace Lily

The Peace Lily is known for its air-purifying qualities and elegant white blooms. It thrives in low light and requires minimal maintenance, making it perfect for homes and offices.

Snake Plant

The Snake Plant improves indoor air by removing toxins and producing oxygen at night. Its tall, striking leaves make it an ideal decorative and health-boosting houseplant.

Monstera

The Monstera, also called the "Swiss Cheese Plant", features unique split leaves. It adds a tropical vibe to interiors and grows easily in moderate sunlight and humidity.
